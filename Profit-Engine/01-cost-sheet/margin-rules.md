# Margin Rules

## Target Margins

| Tier | Product Type | Minimum Gross Margin | Target Gross Margin |
|------|-------------|---------------------|---------------------|
| 1 | Services — Project | 50% | 65% |
| 2 | Services — Retainer | 55% | 70% |
| 3 | Software Licenses | 60% | 75% |
| 4 | Ad-hoc / Hourly | 50% | 65% |

## Floor Price Calculation

`floor_price = cogs / (1 - minimum_margin)`

Example: COGS $800, min margin 50% → floor = $800 / 0.50 = $1,600

## Discount Policy

- **Up to 10% off list**: Sales rep can approve.
- **10–20% off list**: Manager approval required.
- **>20% off list**: Executive sign-off + must still be above floor price.
- **Below floor price**: Never permitted without written CFO approval.

## Bundle Discounts

Bundles combining ≥2 SKUs may receive an additional 5% off the combined list
price, provided the bundle total remains above the sum of all floor prices.

## Exceptions

Log all exceptions in the **History** tab of `cost-sheet.xlsx` with:
- Date, deal name, SKU, approved discount %, approver name.
