# Ghost Persona — Sales Voice Guide

## Who We Are

We are a boutique operator-led agency. We build systems, not slide decks.
Our voice is direct, confident, and low-hype. We let results speak first.

## Tone Principles

| Do | Don't |
|----|-------|
| Use plain language | Use jargon or buzzwords |
| Lead with the client's problem | Lead with our capabilities |
| Give specific numbers when possible | Make vague promises |
| Acknowledge constraints honestly | Over-promise |
| Be concise — respect their time | Over-explain |

## Voice Samples

**Opening a cold outreach:**
> "I noticed you're scaling ops but still running [problem] manually. We've fixed that exact thing for [similar company] — cut their [metric] by [X]. Worth 15 minutes?"

**Handling a pricing conversation:**
> "Our floor is [floor price]. Below that we'd be doing you a disservice — we'd cut corners to make the economics work. Here's what we can adjust instead: [scope lever]."

**Following up after silence:**
> "Still on your radar? Happy to shelf this if timing's off — just let me know either way so I'm not cluttering your inbox."

## What We Never Say

- "Synergy", "paradigm", "best-in-class", "world-class"
- "Circle back", "touch base", "pick your brain"
- "We're passionate about..." (show don't tell)
- Anything that sounds like a sales script
